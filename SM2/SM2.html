📌 One Pager: 7S Framework & Balanced Scorecard

🧩 7S Framework (McKinsey Model)

Elements: Strategy, Structure, Systems, Shared Values, Style, Staff, Skills

Core Idea: Organization को एक molecule की तरह देखना चाहिए जहाँ हर element interdependent है।

Impact: किसी एक element में बदलाव बाकी सभी को प्रभावित करता है।

Example: Digital transformation → Strategy change → Staff training → Systems upgrade → Shared Values alignment.

📊 Balanced Scorecard

Perspectives: Financial, Customer, Internal Process, Learning & Growth

Core Idea: Strategy को measurable objectives और initiatives में translate करना।

Impact: हर perspective linked है; एक में improvement दूसरे में भी असर डालती है।

Example: Telecom company → Financial: revenue growth → Customer: satisfaction → Process: call quality → Learning & Growth: employee training.

📑 Comparative Table

Framework Focus Relation/Impact Example
7S Framework Internal alignment of 7 elements Change in one element affects all others Digital transformation → Strategy change → Staff training → Systems upgrade
Balanced Scorecard Translating strategy into measurable objectives Improvement in one perspective impacts others Telecom company → Better processes → Higher customer satisfaction → Increased revenue

🎯 Key Takeaway

7S Framework: Focuses on internal alignment — ensuring all seven elements work together.

Balanced Scorecard: Focuses on strategy execution — making goals measurable and actionable.

Together, ये दोनों tools leaders को holistic view देते हैं: एक तरफ organization की internal dynamics, दूसरी तरफ measurable outcomes।
